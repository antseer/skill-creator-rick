# Yield Desk — Business Spec

## Goal
Compare CEX and DeFi stablecoin earning products and recommend the most suitable yield opportunity for the user context.

## Rules
- Hero must state top recommendation and why.
- CEX and DeFi can coexist, but recommendation logic must normalize them first.
- Safety, convenience, capacity, stability, and yield all contribute to ranking.
- Every recommendation must include explicit risk and method notes.
